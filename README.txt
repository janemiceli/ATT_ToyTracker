ATT_ToyTracker (zip-ready)

Install:
1) Unzip into: World of Warcraft/_retail_/Interface/AddOns/
2) Ensure 'All The Things' (ATT) is enabled, then enable 'ATT Toy Tracker'
3) In-game: /atttoys ui
   - Click Scan
   - Click Export All or Export Missing
   - Copy from the Export Output box

Notes:
- Locations are best-effort based on fields present in ATT nodes (coord/coords/maps).
- Data persists in WTF/.../SavedVariables/ATT_ToyTracker.lua
